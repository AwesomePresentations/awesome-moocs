## __Computer Networking__ <img width="30" src="http://innovativecomputersolutions.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/it-networking-services-company.png">

Have you ever wondered how the internet works? What's underneath HTTP? How do you keep your web-browsing data safe from a man-in-the-middle attack?

First of all, choose a learning track from the following: 

1. [Web & Software Developer Track](https://github.com/Tapia17/awesome-moocs#web--software-developer-track-): If you are a web developer, knowing networking protocols will help you develop faster and more reliable web apps. This track is made just for you to learn just enough networking knowledge to up your productivity. 
  
2. [Network Administrator/Engineer Track](https://github.com/Tapia17/awesome-moocs#network-administratorengineer-track-): If you are an aspiring network administrator/engineer, this track will help you master the [Networking Fundamentals](https://github.com/Tapia17/awesome-moocs#core1-networking-fundamentals), and a specialization in [Cyber Security](https://github.com/Tapia17/awesome-moocs#core2-cyber-security).

Last but not least, you can consider getting [certified](https://github.com/Tapia17/awesome-moocs#certifications-) after taking these courses.
